Our Story

Born in Thrissur.
Trusted across India.

For over 25 years, Ultima Notebooks has been a name synonymous with quality and trust in the Indian stationery market. Born in Kizhur, Kunnamkulam — we have grown from a local manufacturer into a brand trusted by schools, colleges, and professionals across the country.

Every notebook that leaves our facility carries a commitment to craft. From the selection of 70 GSM paper to the precision of our binding, we believe the tools of learning deserve the same care as learning itself.
